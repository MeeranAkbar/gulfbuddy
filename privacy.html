Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Who We Are

GulfHabibi is a UAE-based information and classifieds platform operated under an Ajman Freezone trade licence with ecommerce activity. Our website is gulfhabibi.com. For any privacy matters, contact us at hello@gulfhabibi.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to provide our services:

  • Account information — name and email address when you register
  • Listing information — details you voluntarily provide when posting an ad or listing
  • Usage data — pages visited, search queries (anonymous, for improving the platform)
  • Contact details — phone number if provided in a listing or service request

We do NOT collect: passport copies, Emirates ID, financial details, or any sensitive personal documents.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your listings and connect you with buyers, sellers, or service providers
  • To send service updates or important notices (not marketing spam)
  • To improve the platform based on usage patterns
  • To comply with UAE laws and regulations

4. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud database with row-level security). We use Google OAuth for authentication — your Google account data is managed by Google under their privacy policy. We do not store your Google password or sensitive Google account information.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data to any third party. We do not share your information with advertisers. Your contact details in listings are visible to other users only because you chose to include them publicly.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal cookies necessary for the platform to function (session management). We may use Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns — this is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

7. Your Rights

Under UAE data protection law, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request deletion of your account and data, and object to certain uses. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@gulfhabibi.com.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions: hello@gulfhabibi.com
