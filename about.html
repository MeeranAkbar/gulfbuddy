About GulfHabibi

GulfHabibi is a free UAE platform built to help residents, expats and businesses connect. Whether you want to buy or sell, find a trusted service provider, get a job or understand your rights under UAE law — GulfHabibi is here for you.

We built GulfHabibi because we saw UAE residents — especially expats — struggling to find reliable, honest information and trusted local businesses. Our mission is simple: make UAE life easier for everyone, for free.
